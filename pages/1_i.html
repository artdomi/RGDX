At what voltage does it glow?

Let's get to our first actual experiment...

Have you tried tuning on the electromagnet while the plasma's on?

Before we begin, make sure you're familiar with the voltage and pressure control.

As you saw in "How to make it glow?" as we increase the voltage the plasma turns on.

The question arises...at what voltage does the plasma turn on?

  • Turn off the light and set the electrode voltage and the electromagnet voltage to zero

  • Set the pressure at 40 mTorr and wait until it stabilizes.


  • Slowly turn the electrode voltage on and note at what voltage the plasma turns on

  • Now, turn the electrode voltage to zero and increase the pressure to 400mTorr.

  • Repeat step 3, that is, slowly turn the electrode voltage up and note the voltage at which the plasma turns on.

    • What did you observe?

    The voltage at which the plasma is ignited (also called the Breakdown Voltage), depends strongly on the pressure of the gas.
